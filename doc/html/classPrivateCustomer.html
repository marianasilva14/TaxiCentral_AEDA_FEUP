§ getDiscount()

float PrivateCustomer::getDiscount ( )
virtual

Function that through the points, get the discounts. If the points are less than 50, the customer doesn't have discount. If the points are more than 50 and less than 100, the discount is 0,1 percent. If the points are more than 100 and less than 150, the discount is 0,2 percent. If the points are more than 150 and less than 200, the discount is 0,3 percent. If the points are more than 200 and less than 250, the discount is 0,4 percent. If the points are more than 250, the discount is 0,5 percent.
